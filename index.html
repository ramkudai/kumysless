About КумысЛесс

We bring the ancient healing tradition of Kazakh fermented mare's milk to modern consumers — pure, natural, and crafted with respect for our ancestors' wisdom.

🐎

Our Heritage

Kumys has been produced by nomadic Kazakh tribes for over 2,500 years. Our producers use traditional fermentation methods passed down through generations on the open steppe.

🌿

Natural & Pure

No additives, no preservatives. Our kumys is made from milk of free-roaming mares, fermented naturally with live Lactobacillus cultures for maximum probiotic benefit.

🚚

Fast Delivery

Refrigerated delivery across all major Kazakh cities. Orders placed before 12:00 are dispatched same day to ensure freshness reaches you at peak quality.

🔬

Lab Certified

Every batch undergoes quality testing at our Almaty laboratory. Certified by Kazakhstan's National Center of Expertise for food safety and microbiological standards.
